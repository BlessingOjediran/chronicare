About Chronicare

Integrated Diabetes Management Centre

Since 2018, Chronicare has helped hundreds of people living with Diabetes to live their best lives through our personalised care programs. Our clinics offer a warm and comfortable setting for you to interact with our doctors, Diabetes Nurse Educators and other specialists and get answers to the questions you may have about your condition. Our personalised approach to chronic disease management empowers you with the tools, support, and confidence you need to live your best life. We offer a one-stop shop experience for People Living with Diabetes, with their access to all required care, Medication, and nutrition.
